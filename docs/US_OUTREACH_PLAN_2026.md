# Petkovic Solutions: US Outreach Plan

Prepared for March 25, 2026.

## Positioning

Primary market:
- US service businesses

Best-fit niches for current portfolio:
- Beauty and med spa style businesses
- Transport, moving, and local services
- Real estate teams and property brands

Primary offer:
- Conversion-focused websites and landing pages

Secondary add-on:
- Lightweight AI assistant for FAQ handling or lead capture

## Offer ladder

1. Free website audit
- The easiest first ask
- Useful for cold outreach because commitment is low

2. Landing page project
- Best for prospects running ads, outbound, or a single focused offer

3. Full business website
- Best for service businesses with weak credibility or poor structure

4. Optional add-ons
- FAQ assistant
- Lead capture chatbot
- Small ongoing update / maintenance retainer

## Recommended pricing

### Package 1: Audit Sprint
- Price: free for outreach, or $150 to $300 as a paid standalone option later
- Deliverable: short Loom or written audit with top issues and recommended direction
- Goal: start conversations and qualify buyers

### Package 2: Landing Page
- Price: $1,200 to $2,000
- Best for: ads, one service offer, one campaign, one niche page
- Scope:
  - One custom landing page
  - Clear CTA structure
  - Contact form or inquiry flow
  - Mobile optimization

### Package 3: Core Website
- Price: $3,000 to $5,000
- Best for: service businesses needing stronger positioning and trust
- Scope:
  - 5 to 7 pages
  - Homepage, service pages, about, contact
  - Clear messaging structure
  - Mobile-first build

### Package 4: Premium Website
- Price: $5,000 to $6,500
- Best for: businesses that need a stronger custom presentation and more strategic structure
- Scope:
  - Full custom website
  - Stronger page hierarchy
  - Copy direction
  - Visual polish
  - Better trust and inquiry flow

### Add-ons
- AI FAQ or lead-capture widget: $300 to $1,000
- Monthly maintenance: $100 to $300 / month

## Outreach operating model

### Weekly rhythm
- Monday to Friday
- 30 to 50 new prospects per day
- 150 to 250 new prospects per week

### Monthly volume
- 600 to 1,000 highly targeted contacts per month
- 1,200 is possible later, but not recommended at the start if list quality drops

### Prospect filtering
- Outdated or weak website
- Poor mobile experience
- Weak trust signals
- No clear CTA
- Niches where first impression matters

### Best outreach asset
- A short personalized email with one concrete observation
- Offer a free website audit, not a full sales pitch

## Cold email sequence

### Email 1
- Day 0
- Mention one concrete problem on the site
- Offer a quick audit

### Email 2
- Day 2 or 3
- Add one more observation
- Keep the ask small

### Email 3
- Day 6 or 7
- Mention that you recorded or can send a short direction note

### Email 4
- Day 10 to 14
- Short final follow-up

## Recommended first move

Start with this:
- Pick 2 niches only
- Send 30 personalized emails per day
- Offer only one thing: a free website audit
- Use the current site as proof and send Noor / Marko as live examples
- Aim to close the first projects in the $3,000 to $4,500 range
